There are 3 gamemodes in Apex Legends. The three gamemodes are: Battleroyale, Arena and ranked.

The first gamemode is Battle Royale. This gamemode is in the game since the release and is the most popular. You can play this mode with a party of 2 or 3 people. At the start of the game, you spawn in a airplane with 60 people's and you have to jump out of a airplane and land on a place to loot for weapons, armor and healing. If your survive the game, you win.

The second gamemode is Arena. In this gamemode, you have 2 teams of 3 people. The purpose is to kill all 3 people of the enemy team in a small map. You also get money every round to buy a weapon, healing or abilitys. After winning 3 rounds, the victory is yours!

The last mode is ranked. This gamemode is almost the same as battle royale but it's with points. You get points for killing people and winning. With more points, you get to a higher rank. after a season, you get a reward depending on your rank. The ranks are: bronze silver gold platinum diamond master predator. The predator rank is the hardest to get and only 750 people in the world can get the predator rank every season.
