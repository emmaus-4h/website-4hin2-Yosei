There are 3 gamemodes in Apex Legends. The three gamemodes are: Battleroyale, Arena and ranked.

The first gamemode is Battle Royale. This gamemode is in the game since the release and is the most popular. You can play this mode with a party of 2 or 3 people. At the start of the game, you spawn in a airplane with 60 people's and you have to jump out of a airplane and land on a place to loot for weapons, armor and healing. If your survive the game, you win.
